## Primary Colors

**Ruby Red** (Main Brand Color)
- HEX: `#CC342D`
- RGB: `204, 52, 45`
- Tailwind: `bg-[#CC342D]`

**Deep Ruby** (Darker variant)
- HEX: `#9B2226`
- RGB: `155, 34, 38`
- Tailwind: `bg-[#9B2226]`

## Secondary Colors

**Coral** (Accent)
- HEX: `#EE6C4D`
- RGB: `238, 108, 77`
- Tailwind: `bg-[#EE6C4D]`

**Champagne** (Light accent)
- HEX: `#F4E8C1`
- RGB: `244, 232, 193`
- Tailwind: `bg-[#F4E8C1]`

## Neutral Colors

**Charcoal** (Text)
- HEX: `#2D3142`
- RGB: `45, 49, 66`
- Tailwind: `bg-[#2D3142]`

**Slate Gray** (Secondary text)
- HEX: `#6B7280`
- RGB: `107, 114, 128`
- Tailwind: `bg-gray-500`

**Light Gray** (Backgrounds)
- HEX: `#F3F4F6`
- RGB: `243, 244, 246`
- Tailwind: `bg-gray-100`

**White** (Surfaces)
- HEX: `#FFFFFF`
- RGB: `255, 255, 255`
- Tailwind: `bg-white`

## Usage Guide

- **Primary Actions:** Ruby Red (#CC342D)
- **Hover States:** Deep Ruby (#9B2226)
- **Links & Highlights:** Coral (#EE6C4D)
- **Backgrounds:** Light Gray (#F3F4F6) & White (#FFFFFF)
- **Text:** Charcoal (#2D3142) for headings, Slate Gray (#6B7280) for body
